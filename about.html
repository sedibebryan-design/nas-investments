EL +01 · About us

Legitimacy, on every site instruction.

A 100% black-owned, BEE Level 1 contractor offering a complete, turnkey construction solution — under one appointment, not a chain of subcontractors.

NAS Investment & Solution Trading is a 100% black-owned company holding BEE Level 1 status, offering comprehensive construction services across South Africa. We're built as an all-inclusive contractor spanning multiple disciplines, so a single appointment covers a complete, turnkey solution — not a chain of subcontractors.

Our objective is straightforward: deliver cost-effective work that meets the client's, the engineer's, and the local authority's specifications, on time and with complete legitimacy at every stage.

Our growth rests on a proven ability to adapt to the unique, shifting, and often demanding development needs of the markets we build in.

Vision, mission & values

Vision

To be the pre-eminent leader in infrastructure development, owning and operating vital parts of the construction supply chain in a profitable, growth-oriented way.

Mission

To develop, build and service Africa's needs for better living, working and travelling conditions, raising the standard of civil and construction quality with skilled, competitive teams.

Values

Innovation delivered through legitimacy; a lateral leadership style built on trust; and a belief that loyalty is what secures a sustainable future for our people.
