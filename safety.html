EL +04 · Environment & safety

Safety is delegated, never optional.

From the Managing Director down to every site worker, health, safety and environmental responsibility run through everything we build.

Health & Safety

OHS Act compliance, every day

We adhere to the Occupational Health and Safety Act (Act No. 85 of 1993) and insist on qualified, accredited supervisory staff holding valid First Aid certificates.

Safety starts with the Managing Director and is delegated down through Senior Managers, Project Managers and Safety Officers to every member of staff — keeping the operating environment safe for our people and the public.

Environment

An obligation, not a choice

We believe the quality of the air, water and soil should be protected for the continued benefit of all ecosystems, and we act responsibly with regard to the impact of our operations.

Taking care of the environment is an obligation — not a choice.

Accredited supervision

Qualified supervisory staff with valid First Aid certificates oversee every active site.

Environmental care

Operations and services are run with deliberate regard for air, water and soil quality.

Delegated responsibility

Safety accountability flows from the Managing Director through every level of staff.
